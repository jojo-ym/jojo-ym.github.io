SAMSUNG GALAXY A54 5G

The Samsung Galaxy A54 5G is a mid-range smartphone that offers a balance of performance, display quality, and features, making it an excellent choice for users who want a solid experience without spending flagship prices.

Features

  • Display: Size: 6.4-inch Super AMOLED display, Full HD+ (1080 x 2340 pixels).
  • Design and Build: Sleek and comfortable to hold, IP67 certified for water and dust resistance.
  • Connectivity: 5G Support: Fast data speeds in 5G-enabled areas, Wi-Fi 6 for faster wireless internet speeds.
  • Camera: 50MP, f/1.8 (wide), with OIS (Optical Image Stabilization), 12MP, f/2.2 for wide-angle shots.
  • Other Features: Stereo Speakers with Dolby Atmos support for immersive sound.

Design & Build

Sleek and comfortable to hold ,and IP67 certified for water and dust resistance. Gorilla Glass 5 for protection on both front and back for durability.

Performance

Exynos 1380 (5nm) for solid performance,Android 13 with One UI 5.1, offering a clean and customizable user interface, and have a 6GB or 8GB, ideal for multitasking.
